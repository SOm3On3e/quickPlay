How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

🖱️

Click to Place

Players take turns clicking empty squares to place their X or O mark.

3️⃣

Three in a Row

Get three of your marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win!

🏆

Score Tracking

Scores are tracked across rounds. The game auto-restarts after each round ends.
