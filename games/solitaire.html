How to Play Klondike Solitaire

🃏

The Setup

7 tableau columns, 4 foundation piles (top right), and a stock pile (top left). Click the stock to draw a card.

⬆️

Build Foundations

Move cards to foundations by suit, starting with Ace up to King. Fill all 4 piles to win!

🔴🖤

Tableau Rules

Stack cards in descending order, alternating red and black suits. Click a card to select, then click a valid destination.
