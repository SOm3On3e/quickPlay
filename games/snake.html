How to Play Snake

⬆⬇⬅➡

Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD on desktop. Use the D-pad below the game on mobile.

🍎

Objective

Eat the glowing red dots to grow your snake and increase your score. Don't run into walls or yourself!

⚡

Tips

The snake speeds up every 5 points. Plan your path ahead — don't get trapped in a corner.
