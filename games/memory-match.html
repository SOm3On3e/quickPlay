How to Play Memory Match

👆

Click to Flip

Click any face-down card to reveal its emoji. Then click a second card.

🔄

Match Pairs

If both cards show the same emoji, they stay face-up! If not, they flip back over.

🏆

Clear the Board

Match all 8 pairs in as few moves as possible. Challenge yourself to beat your best!
