❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Are all games really free?

Yes, 100% free — no premium tiers, no paywalls, no hidden charges. We keep the lights on through optional donations and sponsored blog content (clearly labeled).

Can I suggest a game to add?

Absolutely! Select "Game Suggestion" in the form above and tell us what you'd like to play. We read every suggestion and add popular requests to our roadmap.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Select "Bug Report" in the form, choose the affected game, and describe what happened. Include your browser and device type if possible — it helps us fix it faster.

Do you accept guest blog posts?

Yes! Select "Blog Post Pitch" and share your topic idea. We look for original, well-researched articles about browser gaming, strategy, or game design. No AI-spun content.

How do I submit my own game?

Use our dedicated game submission form for the fastest review. We accept HTML5 / JavaScript games that run in a browser without plugins.

How long does it take to hear back?

General inquiries: 24–48 hours. Bug reports: usually same-day triage. Game submissions: up to 5 business days. Press inquiries: within 24 hours.
