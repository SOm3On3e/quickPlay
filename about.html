🎯 Our Mission

Why We Exist

We believe a good game should be as easy to start as turning on a light switch. No app stores. No accounts. No credit cards. No "install to continue." Just click and play — the way browser gaming was always meant to be.

— The QuickPlay Team, 2024
⚡

Speed First

Every game on QuickPlay is built or vetted to load in under 3 seconds on a standard connection. No bloated engines, no unnecessary assets. Fast is a feature.

🚫

No Nonsense

No ads in the play area. No forced sign-ups. No "watch a video to continue." We monetize through optional donations and sponsored blog content — never through disrupting your game.

📱

Works Everywhere

Every game is tested on mobile, tablet, and desktop before going live. Touch controls, responsive layouts, and keyboard support — no player left behind.

🌍

Open Platform

Developers can submit their games for free. We believe indie game creators deserve a platform where they can reach real players without paying for discovery.

♿

Accessible

We follow WCAG accessibility guidelines — proper ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, and screen reader compatibility across all pages.

🔒

Privacy Respecting

We don't sell your data. We don't track you across the web. The only data we store locally is your game scores — and only in your own browser's storage.

📅 Timeline

How We Got Here

January 2024
The Idea

Alex and Jake sketch out the concept during a 6-hour flight. Goal: a gaming site that loads faster than the snack trolley arrives.

March 2024
First 10 Games Launch

QuickPlay goes live with 10 games and zero marketing. Word of mouth drives 12,000 visitors in the first week.

July 2024
Sara & Maya Join

The blog launches. The first post — "Top 10 Games to Play When Bored" — hits 80K readers in a month.

December 2024
1 Million Players

QuickPlay crosses 1 million monthly active players. We celebrate by adding 10 more games and finally sleeping.

2026 — Now
50+ Games & Growing

2 million monthly players, 50+ games, a thriving blog, and an open submission platform for indie developers. Just getting started.
